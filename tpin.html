Project: Graphics/Social Media Content
Where: The Public Interest Network in Boston, Massachusetts
When: January - May 2018
Description: While at The Public Interest Network, I was tasked with creating graphics that were meant to evoke action in the reciprocants of the images. I used Canva for all of the designing. Along with the graphics, I was responsible with writing news network updates that circulated within the company to update different departments on what we were working on. I gained a lot of written and verbal communication skills from this internship and I better understand how to get my ideas across.
